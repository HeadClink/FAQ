What does HeadClink do exactly?

We connect presenters around the world by real-time video or audio chats to get feedback from the evaluators. This means that you would be delivering your speech, presentation, etc to a similar audience before your actual audience, resulting in better performance.

In the case of not connecting with a live evaluator, we also have a forum where you could post a recording of your video. You would get feedback from many evaluators over a time period of your choosing.

What do I get as an evaluator?

You can earn cash through the ClinkBucks or rewards, as an evaluator. You could also use the ClinkBucks to buy paid services on the platform.

What do I get as an presenter?

You get the opportunity to practice and receive valuable feedback on your presentations/speeches before facing the judging audience.

Do I have to pay to use the service?

Essentially no. You do not have to pay to use the platform as many of our features are free. However, we do have subscription packages and a few items that you could buy if you enjoy using our platform and want to upgrade.

Who can use this?

It is targeted towards students in any field although anyone with a need to practice presenting can use it!
